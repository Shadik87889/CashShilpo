CashShilpo is an all-in-one Point of Sale (POS) software designed for small to medium-sized businesses. It's perfect for retail stores, cafes, restaurants, and service-based businesses that need to manage sales, inventory, customers, and analytics in one place.

CashShilpo works on any device with a modern web browser, including PCs, Macs, iPads, and Android tablets. It's compatible with most standard POS hardware like receipt printers (Epson, Star), barcode scanners, and cash drawers.
